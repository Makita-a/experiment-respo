About exploring

One of my favourite things to do is explore. Whether it's discovering a hidden café, walking through a new neighbourhood, visiting a museum, hiking outdoors, or trying a restaurant I've never been to — I enjoy experiencing new places and cultures.


Food exploration has become one of my biggest hobbies. There's something about sitting down somewhere unfamiliar and trying a dish you've never heard of — it's exciting every single time. Over the years I've worked my way through a long list of cuisines, and the list keeps growing.


There's always another place to discover and another dish to try. What should I try next? If you have recommendations, I'm always listening.
