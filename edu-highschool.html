About my school

I studied at Oxford International School in Dhaka from kindergarten all the way through to Grade 12 — so it's safe to say this place shaped a huge part of who I am. I completed both Cambridge O Levels and A Levels here.


It was the kind of school that pushed you to think broadly, not just within one subject. By the time I reached A Levels, I had found real passions — and also the beginning of a direction I wasn't expecting.
