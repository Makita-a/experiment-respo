About my cooking

Cooking is one of my favourite creative outlets. I especially enjoy cooking dishes that my parents don't usually make at home — for me, the kitchen is another form of exploration: learning new techniques, experimenting with flavours, and sharing food with others.


I grew up with two cuisines at home — Bangladeshi and Japanese — and that mix shaped both my palate and the way I think about food. Now I'll happily try cooking almost anything.

Highlight
November 2024 — Cooking Class
Teaching 20 people to cook, all in 2 hours

Last November, I organised and taught a cooking class for around 20 participants. We made three dishes from scratch — all within two hours. It was chaotic, fun, and genuinely one of my favourite memories.
